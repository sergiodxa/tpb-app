The Pirate Bay WebApp

This WebApp allows you to search The Pirate Bay and get magnetlinks of torrents without having to enter the site.

This WebApp born due to blockade of The Pirate Bay produced in Argentina by ISP companies: Speedy, Movistar, Fibertel, Prima, Telecentro Iplan Arnet, Personal, Metrotel, Sion and Claro under the orders of the CNC.
